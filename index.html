Care & Wash Instructions

Your handcrafted accessories require a little extra love to keep them looking pristine.

Bags & Clutches (Rainbow Bag, White Shoulder Bag, Rose Clutch)

  • Cleaning: Spot clean gently with a damp cloth and mild detergent. Do not vigorously rub.
  • Drying: Lay flat to dry naturally. Do not machine dry, as it may warp the shape.
  • Storage: Stuff the bags with tissue paper when not in use to maintain their structure.

Home Decor (Star Mats)

  • Washing: Hand wash gently in cool water.
  • Drying: Pin flat to a towel to ensure it dries in a perfect star shape.

Hair Accessories (Headbands, Clips & Bandanas)

  • Washing: Hand wash gently in cool water with a mild soap.
  • Drying: Lay flat on a clean towel to dry out of direct sunlight.

Keychains & Hardware (Evil Eye)

  • Cleaning: Wipe gently with a dry or lightly damp cloth. Keep away from harsh chemicals and perfumes to protect the metal hardware.
